Toad!

Toad is a character who is Princess Peach's attendant and a long time protector of the Mushroom Kingdom.

Toad is often foreseen as a happy character (happy almost to the point of childishness). He is presumably one of the most humble characters in the Mario series, as he seems to be actually aware of his own cowardice. Despite this, Toad is kind and often helps Mario on his multiple adventures.
